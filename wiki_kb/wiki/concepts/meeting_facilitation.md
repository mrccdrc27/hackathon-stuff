# Meeting Facilitation

The use of AI (via [[entities/gemini_multimodal_live_api.md]]) to moderate team communications.

## Features
- **Time-Boxing:** Keeping agenda items within time limits.
- **Goal Alignment:** Flagging deviations from the critical path.
- **Issue Resolution:** Setting clear "next steps" for blockers.
- Approaches evaluated include Discord bots, Self-Hosted (LiveKit), and Enterprise bots (Recall.ai).
