# Gemini 3.1 Flash Live Preview
**Source:** [[raw/Gemini 3.1 Flash Live Preview    Gemini API.md]]
**Ingested:** 2026-05-05

## Summary
Gemini 3.1 Flash Live Preview is a low-latency, audio-to-audio model optimized for real-time dialogue and voice-first applications. It features acoustic nuance detection and numeric precision.

## Key Takeaways
- **Inputs:** Text, images, audio, video; **Outputs:** Text and audio.
- **Token Limits:** 128k input, 64k output.
- **New Features:** `thinkingLevel` (minimal to high) replaces `thinkingBudget`; multi-part `BidiGenerateContentServerContent` events.
- **Migration:** Move from `gemini-2.5-flash-native-audio-preview-12-2025` to `gemini-3.1-flash-live-preview`.
- **Note:** Proactive audio and affective dialogue are *not yet* supported in 3.1 Flash Live.
